Statement of the Problem

The study investigates the effectiveness of Dalandan (Citrus aurantium) peels as a natural insecticide for cockroaches. It focuses on how cockroaches react to Dalandan peel spray by measuring their reaction time, movement distance, and number of cockroaches that move away from the treated area. The research aims to determine whether the Dalandan peel mixture can successfully repel cockroaches and how effective it is in influencing their behavior.
