Methodology

  • Materials: Dalandan peels, boiled water, measuring tools, spray solution, stopwatch, and live cockroaches.
  • Procedure:Dalandan peels were divided into two groups—crushed and intact—then mixed with water at a 1:2 ratio and boiled. The solutions were sprayed on one side of an observation area.
      • Data Collection: Researchers measured:
    • Time taken for cockroaches to react
    • Distance moved away from the spray
    • Number of cockroaches that avoided the treated area
  • Analysis:Results were recorded, averaged, and analyzed using the ANOVA test, with data presented through tables and graphs.
