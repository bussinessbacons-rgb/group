“Effectiveness and Efficiency of Innovated Mops in Sanitation and Household Cleaning: A Comparative Study”

Research Objectives

  • Assess the effectiveness of the innovative mop compared to a regular mop, particularly in terms of utility, accessibility, and efficiency.
  • Evaluate the overall quality of the innovative mop, including durability, appearance, and features.
  • Analyze and compare the performance of the innovative mop against the traditional mop in household and sanitation use.
