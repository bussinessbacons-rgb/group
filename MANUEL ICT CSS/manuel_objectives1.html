Research Objectives

  • Determine the effectiveness of Annona muricata seed extract in causing mortality among Aedes aegypti mosquito larvae and eggs.
  • Identify whether there is a significant difference in larval mortality between treated groups and the control group.
  • Evaluate the dose-dependent relationship between seed extract concentration and mosquito larval mortality.
  • Develop a prototype eco-friendly larvicidal spray using Annona muricata seed extract that may be applied in school learning areas.
