Recommendations

It is recommended that future studies explore different ratios of cassava starch and chitosan to further improve the mechanical properties of the bioplastic. Additional tests such as water resistance, biodegradation rate, and shelf-life evaluation may also be conducted.

Local government units, schools, and small-scale manufacturers are encouraged to consider the use of cassava-based bioplastics as an alternative to single-use plastics. Further research may also focus on large-scale production and cost-effectiveness to promote sustainable material development in Marikina City.
