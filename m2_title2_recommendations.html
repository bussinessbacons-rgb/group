Recommendations

Future studies should consider monitoring additional factors such as light exposure, pest infestation, water availability, and nutrient deficiencies, as these may influence plant growth results. Longer observation periods are also recommended to evaluate long-term fertilizer effects.

Researchers are encouraged to explore organic bone meal as a sustainable fertilizer alternative due to its slow nutrient release and environmental benefits. Proper seed placement, controlled watering, and consistent evaluation timelines should be followed to ensure accurate results.
