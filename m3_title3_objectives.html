Research Objectives

  • To determine the chemical composition of corn husk in terms of cellulose, lignin, ash, and extractives.
  • To analyze the fiber morphology of corn husk, including fiber length, diameter, lumen width, and wall thickness.
  • To evaluate the suitability of corn husk as an alternative raw material for pulp and paper production.
  • To assess the strength properties of paper sheets produced from corn husk pulp.
