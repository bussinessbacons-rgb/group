Statement of the Problem

This study seeks to explore the functionality and effectiveness of a robotic device designed to locate and assist unconscious individuals. The primary research questions are as follows:

  1. Can orion (Observational Robotic Intervention for Optimized Notifications) device detect individuals/persons when:
    1. In motion
    2. Fall upon fainting
    3. At rest or no motion for some time?
  2. How fast can ORION detect the person's when:
    1. In motion
    2. Fall upon fainting
    3. At rest or no motion for some time?
  3. How fast can Orion send alarm for an emergency call to respond immediately?
  4. Is ORION device efficient in its function?
