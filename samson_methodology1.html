Methodology

1. Essential Oil Extraction:
The manufacturing process begins with extracting essential oils from rose, lemongrass, and sampaguita using solvent extraction. Ensure that the natural properties and therapeutic effects are preserved from the process.
2. Wax Preparation:
Prepare the wax by melting the beeswax using a double boiler and heat it to 185°F. Stir in the extracted essential oil and then turn off the heat and allow the wax mixture to cool to 125°F before pouring.
3. Candle Making:
Prepare a mold that contains a candle wick, then pour the infused wax into the mold. Let cool and the candle is finished
