Methodology

Methods

  • The flashlight is designed as a handheld dual-purpose device.
  • An Arduino microcontroller processes data from:
    • Ultrasonic sensor for depth measurement
    • Turbidity sensor for water clarity classification
  • An LED provides illumination with adjustable brightness.
  • Water depth readings are displayed on an LCD screen.
  • All components are enclosed in a flashlight casing.
  • The device is powered by a rechargeable battery.
  • The experiment is conducted indoors using a plastic container.
  • Water depths from 5 cm to 25 cm are tested.
  • Three water conditions are prepared:
    • Clear water
    • Low turbidity (soil added)
    • High turbidity (more soil added)
  • The flashlight is kept at a fixed height above the water surface.
  • A ruler is used to record the actual water depth for comparison.
