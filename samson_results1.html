Results

The candle burn time ranges from 3 hours and 34 minutes to 10 hours and 20 minutes, with longer wicks correlating to longer burn durations. A notable 75% of respondents enjoyed the scent, although some found it weak, possibly due to uneven fragrance distribution during wax melting. The candles were visually appealing, featuring smooth surfaces and smaller sizes than typical commercial candles. Respondents appreciated the pleasant and peaceful atmosphere created by the candles, which evoked scents reminiscent of orchards and forests. Durability varied by candle type; Sampaguita candles melted easily, Lemongrass candles had good longevity at nearly 11 hours, while feedback on Rose candles was mixed regarding burn time. Improvements could be made in wax hardness or wick placement to enhance durability.
