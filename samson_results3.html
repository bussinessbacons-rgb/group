Results

  • The floodwater-depth sensing flashlight is expected to measure water depth accurately and consistently compared to a standard ruler.
  • The device is expected to perform best in clear and low-turbidity water, with only minor errors in highly turbid conditions.
  • Flashlight brightness is expected to have no significant effect on measurement accuracy.
  • Most test results are expected to show no significant difference between flashlight readings and actual water depth (p > 0.05).
